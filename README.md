A collection of **completely fictional** Monte Carlo war-gaming simulators that happen to use real-world defense modeling techniques. For educational purposes only. Probably.

## Current Simulations

### 🚀 Operation Kaboom — Missile Force Attrition Simulator
Models the degradation of a *hypothetical* nation's missile strike capability under sustained air campaign pressure. Features:
- TEL (Transporter Erector Launcher) survival decay via binomial attrition
- Production bottleneck modeling (when 95% of your factories are "renovated")
- Salvo bandwidth collapse — the moment you go from "shock and awe" to "please and thank you"
- Monte Carlo confidence intervals (1000 runs, because one apocalypse isn't enough)
